Focus Areas

Please see the following pages for additional role information grouped by position focus

Production Operator: Casting

Operate and monitor casting equipment to produce high-quality aluminum products while ensuring safety, efficiency, and adherence to quality and industry standards.

  • Casting 1 and 2

Production Operator: Senior Caster

Lead and oversee casting operations, ensuring high-quality aluminum production, optimizing processes, and mentoring team members while maintaining strict standards.

  • Senior Caster

Production Operator: Charging

Manage and execute the preparation and charging of raw materials into melting furnaces, ensuring efficient aluminum production while adhering to safety and quality protocols.

  • Charging 1 and 2

Production Operator: Saw

Operate and maintain saw equipment to cut aluminum products to precise specifications, ensuring quality, efficiency, and adherence to safety and industry standards.

  • Saw 1 and 2

Material Handling

Handle the loading, unloading, and transportation of raw materials and finished aluminum products, ensuring efficient material flow while maintaining safety and quality standards.

  • Loading and Unloading

Material Processing

Perform and oversee material processing operations, including melting and tipping aluminum, to ensure efficient production, quality standards, and compliance with safety protocols.

  • Tipper and Melter

Front Office

Coordinate administrative tasks, customer interactions, and operational support to ensure efficient communication, scheduling, and documentation in a fast-paced aluminum processing environment.

  • Receiving Specialist

Leadership Positions

Leadership positions focus on guiding teams, optimizing processes, and driving safety, quality, and production efficiency while fostering continuous improvement and employee development.

  • Leads, Supervisors, and Managers
