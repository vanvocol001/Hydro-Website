Front Office:

Shipping Receiving Specialist

"The employee needs intermediate computer skills, ability to multitask, and good communication skills with vendors and customers."

The Front Office: Shipping & Receiving Specialist is responsible for coordinating and managing shipping and receiving activities while ensuring accurate documentation, timely processing, and adherence to company standards. This role combines administrative duties with operational support, requiring excellent time management, problem-solving, and communication skills to address non-routine issues and maintain seamless operations.

Key responsibilities include scheduling shipments, receiving deliveries, managing records, and providing backup support for shipping and receiving operations. The role requires intermediate computer skills, including proficiency with MS Office, APICS, and SAP, to process transactions, track inventory, and generate reports. Basic math skills are essential for measuring dimensions, calculating percentages, and ensuring accuracy in quality control metrics.

The ideal candidate demonstrates reliability under pressure, a results-oriented mindset, and the ability to adapt to changing priorities. Strong teamwork and communication skills are critical, as this position involves collaboration with internal teams, carriers, and vendors. A focus on continuous improvement and the ability to implement basic enhancements to processes are key to success in this role.
