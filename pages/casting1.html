Casting I&II

In Casting I, to be successful, an employee needs to have strong attention skills--to be able to understand table conditions and to keep them in good working condition. It is important for the person in this role to have a strong sense of urgency. In Casting II, an employee needs to be able to see the bigger picture--having an understanding of the casting process, and stay ahead of Charging. The employee needs a strong sense of urgency, ability and willingness to follow directives, and most importantly willingness to help others in area as needed.
